# Metadata Standard

Every permanent note should contain:

```yaml
---
title:

created:

updated:

type:

domain:

status:

tags:

aliases:

related:

source:

confidence:
---
```

Types

Allowed:

Observation

Knowledge

Principle

Idea

Research

Project

Decision

Postmortem

Philosophy

Journal

Specification

---

Status

Allowed:

draft

active

evergreen

archived
